About Us

We are a team of weather enthusiasts who are passionate about providing accurate and reliable weather information to people all around the world.

Our mission is to make it easy for people to access weather information that they can trust, whether they are planning a trip, scheduling an outdoor event, or just trying to decide what to wear in the morning.

If you have any questions or feedback about our weather app, please don't hesitate to contact us.
